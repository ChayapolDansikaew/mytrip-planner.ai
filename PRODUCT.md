# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Travelers planning trips (both local and international) who want quick, personalized travel itineraries without the high cognitive load of manual research. They access the app during high-excitement planning phases, using mobile devices on the go or desktop displays at home.

## Product Purpose
To generate highly tailored, structured travel itineraries using generative AI, visualize them interactively on dynamic Mapbox maps, and manage user data securely in real-time. Success is defined by the immediate clarity of the trip plan, friction-free map exploration, and inspiring visual design.

## Brand Personality
Playful, Cheerful, Inspiring. Warm, welcoming, and adventurous. It should feel like a reliable digital travel companion that sparks excitement for the journey ahead.

## Anti-references
- Saturated AI-generated SaaS templates
- Thick side-stripe card borders (e.g., `border-l-4` indicators)
- Generic metric/stat dashboard grids
- Overly complex layouts with heavy royal blue or standard red primary components
- Decorative glassmorphism and tacky elastic bounce animations

## Design Principles
1. **Map-First Spatial Intuition**: The map and geographical context must work hand-in-hand with the timeline, ensuring the physical journey is visually intuitive.
2. **Playful Simplicity**: Inject delight using warm, cheerful colors, friendly rounded geometry, and soft transitions without adding unnecessary layout clutter or visual noise.
3. **Zero-Friction Exploration**: Itineraries must be easily read, navigated, and customized, keeping inputs streamlined and outcomes highly visual.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
1. **Contrast & Legibility (WCAG AA)**: Text must maintain clear readability (minimum 4.5:1 ratio) against colored or tinted backgrounds, prioritizing the Kanit typeface.
2. **Reduced Motion**: Respect system preferences by substituting animations with instant transitions or simple crossfades when reduced motion is enabled.
3. **Flexible Breakpoints**: Ensure all components are responsive and adapt elegantly to mobile screen sizes without text overflow or clipping.
